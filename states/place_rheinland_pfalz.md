# Rheinland-Pfalz
## Das Land, das aus drei Stücken zusammengesetzt wurde, die der Fluss immer zusammengehalten hat.

## Owner
- Project: Autobahn

## Shown
The Rhine gorge on the A61 south of Koblenz: the road running the valley floor, the river briefly visible between the hills, the vineyard slopes on both sides. The Loreley rock 200 metres from the road at the relevant exit. On the A61 north through the Hunsrück plateau: flatter, emptier, the road wide and straight over elevated terrain with no view.

The French border on the A35/A6 approach: the road changes character 20 km before it. The signage bilingual.

## Holds
No roads in this project cross Rhineland-Palatinate yet.

## Offers
Mixed terrain - valley and plateau routes have different characters. The Rhine gorge section among the most scenically positioned Autobahn in Germany. Moderate traffic outside the Frankfurt and Cologne approach roads. Unrestricted on plateau sections; speed limits in the valley where the road curves.

## Withheld
The Loreley: the rock and the Rhine bend visible for approximately 6 seconds at motorway speed from the A61. The Moselle wine valley: entirely absent from the motorway - the A1 crosses it once at Schweich, briefly. The Nürburgring: 30 km from the A61 at Adenau, not visible from any motorway.
