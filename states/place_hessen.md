# Hessen
## Das Land, durch das die Welt auf dem Weg woanders hin fährt.

## Owner
- Project: Autobahn

## Shown
Transit. The signs multiply before Frankfurt is visible. The A5 from the north and the A3 from the east converge at Frankfurter Kreuz - the busiest motorway interchange in Europe. Four motorways meeting. The skyline of Frankfurt visible from the approach roads: the only true European-scale financial district skyline in Germany. The road here is a destination approach and a transit corridor simultaneously.

South of Frankfurt: the road climbing into the Taunus on the A3, descending into the Rhine plain on the A5. The landscape changes faster than in flat north Germany.

## Holds
No roads in this project cross Hesse yet.

## Offers
The highest traffic density per motorway kilometre in Germany. Frequent Stau at Frankfurter Kreuz (A3/A5), Dreieck Mönchhof (A60/A67), and Wiesbadener Kreuz (A3/A66). Heavy freight on all routes. Speed limits through construction zones frequent.

## Withheld
Frankfurt from the motorway approach - the skyline visible but the city not reachable without leaving the road. The ECB towers east of the A3. The Römerberg in Frankfurt's old town: unreachable from the motorway without a 20-minute diversion. The Taunus plateau: visible from the A3 but inaccessible from the carriageway.
