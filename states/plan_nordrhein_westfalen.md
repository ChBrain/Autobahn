# Autobahn Build Plan: Nordrhein-Westfalen

*v1.0 — 03.05.2026*

## Status: PARTIAL

A40 built. NRW has the most complex Autobahn network in Germany: 18+ roads,
the highest traffic density per kilometre, and the post-industrial Ruhr as its backbone.
This is the largest single build task in the entire world.

## Autobahnen

A1, A2, A3, A4, A30, A31, A33, A40, A42, A43, A44, A45, A46, A52, A57, A59, A61 (partial)

## Totals

~130-155 new files. 20-25 sessions.

## Defining Withheld

The Zeche Zollverein in Essen: UNESCO World Heritage, visible as a silhouette from the A40.
The Rhine between Koeln and Dusseldorf: 20 km from the A40, not visible.
The Koelner Dom: 800 metres from Koeln-Zentrum exit, not visible from the carriageway.
What was made in the Ruhr and is no longer made.
