# Niedersachsen
## Das Land, das die Mitte hält ohne es anzukündigen.

## Owner
- Project: Autobahn

## Shown
Flat to gently rolling. The road widening south of Hamburg as the density drops. The Lüneburger Heide in the east - visible as a dark horizon from the A7. Hannover at the centre of the state: the road meeting from four directions. The landscape does not announce itself. It holds.

The A7 runs the full north-south length of the state, from the Hamburg border near Maschen to the Hesse border near Göttingen - the longest uninterrupted motorway run through one state in northern Germany.

## Holds
No roads in this project cross Lower Saxony yet. The A7 southern extension will pass through here.

## Offers
Mixed speed limits - unrestricted on many sections, 130 recommended on others. Heavy truck traffic on the A7 and A2 (E30 east-west corridor). The road surface generally good, some older sections near Hannover. The Autobahn service area density moderate.

## Withheld
Hannover: 12 km from the A7 at the city exits. The Lüneburger Heide on foot or by bike - 5 km from the A7 at several points but not accessible from the carriageway. The Weser river crossings. The Harz mountains visible from the A7 south of Hannover on clear days - not reachable without leaving the motorway.
