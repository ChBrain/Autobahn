# Hamburg
## Die Republik, die der Nation beigetreten ist - ohne sie zu werden.

## Owner
- Project: Autobahn

## Shown
The city-state announced by signs before it is visible. The road narrows to 100 km/h. Junctions multiply. The Lärmschutztunnel - the motorway disappearing into green-covered cuts at Hamburg-Schnelsen and again at Stellingen. The road sound changed. The city above, the road below it, neither fully aware of the other.

The Elbtunnel: the descent from Hamburg-Othmarschen. Daylight gone. Tile walls. 60 km/h. The Elbe 28 metres above. The ascent at the south end into Hamburg south of the river. The port cranes visible briefly from the A7 approach to Waltershof.

## Offers
100 km/h throughout. Dense junctions with short weaving distances. The Elbtunnel mandatory for the A7 crossing - no alternative, 60 km/h, one of the highest-traffic tunnels in Germany. The Lärmschutztunnel sections: the road acoustically contained, exits spaced for urban density.

## Withheld
The port - the third largest in Europe. Reachable from A7 Waltershof. The container terminals not visible from the motorway. The Elbphilharmonie 2 km northeast of the Elbtunnel north portal. The Reeperbahn 1 km east of the A7 at Othmarschen. The Alster lakes in the centre of the city: unreachable from the motorway without leaving it entirely.
