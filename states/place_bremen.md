# Bremen
## Die Sturheit, die die Stadt frei gehalten hat.

## Owner
- Project: Autobahn

## Shown
The city-state approached through Lower Saxony. The A27 enters without preamble - the urban density appearing quickly. The Weser visible briefly from the A27 bridge at the port approach: the cranes of Bremerhaven 60 km north, the container port of Bremen 3 km south of the A27 crossing. The road practical, short, direct. No distance to cover - the state is the city.

## Holds
No roads in this project cross Bremen yet.

## Offers
Short internal distances - the state is two urban areas (Bremen and Bremerhaven) separated by Lower Saxony. The A27 as the main connection. Urban speed limits throughout. The road functional rather than scenic.

## Withheld
The Roland at the Marktplatz - the statue of Roland, symbol of civic freedom and market rights since 1404. Not visible from any motorway. The Beck's brewery chimney at the Hafen: visible from the A1 approach on clear days, the red star identifiable. The Böttcherstraße: 5 minutes on foot from the central exit but requiring full urban navigation to reach.
