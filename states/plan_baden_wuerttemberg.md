# Autobahn Build Plan: Baden-Wuerttemberg

*v1.0 — 03.05.2026*

## Status: STUB

Holds empty. Baden-Wuerttemberg: the Black Forest state, the Stuttgart basin, the Rhine valley.
The road running between the mountains and the plain on the A5 is the most scenically
positioned Autobahn corridor in western Germany.

## Autobahnen

A5, A6, A7, A8, A61, A81, A96, A98
