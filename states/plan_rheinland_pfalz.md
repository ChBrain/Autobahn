# Autobahn Build Plan: Rheinland-Pfalz

*v1.0 — 03.05.2026*

## Status: STUB

Holds empty. Rheinland-Pfalz was assembled from three pieces after 1945 —
the Rhine, the Moselle, the Eifel — and the road only touches the Rhine, briefly.

## Autobahnen

A1, A3, A48, A60, A61, A62, A63, A65

## Phases

### Phase 0 — State file update (now)
The Rhine gorge as the defining moment. The assembled-state identity. Transit character.
Files: 1 updated.

### Phase 1 — A61 RLP (2-3 sessions)
The main north-south spine. The Rhine gorge section south of Koblenz.
The Loreley 200 metres from the road at the relevant exit.
Files: 1 road file + 15-18 exit files.

### Phase 2 — A3 RLP (1-2 sessions)
Frankfurt to Koblenz section. The descent from the Taunus toward the Rhine.

### Phase 3 — A1, A48, A60, A62, A63, A65 (3-4 sessions)
Remaining roads: Trier corridor (A1), Koblenz ring (A48), Mainz approaches (A60, A63),
Pfalz wine country (A65), Kaiserslautern corridor (A62).
Files: 6 road files + 25-30 exit files.

### Phase 4 — State file final (1 session)
Full linked Holds, all 8 roads.
Files: 1 updated.

## Totals

~49-59 new files. 7-10 sessions.

## Defining Withheld

The Loreley: visible for 6 seconds from the A61 at speed.
The Moselle wine valley: the A1 crosses it once at Schweich, briefly.
The Nurburgring: 30 km from the A61 at Adenau. Present in summer as a sound when the wind is right.
Not visible from any motorway.
