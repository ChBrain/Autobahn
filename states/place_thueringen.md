# Thüringen
## Das Erbe, das alles enthält, was die Kultur am meisten schätzt und am meisten fürchtet - auf demselben Hügel.

## Owner
- Project: Autobahn

## Shown
The landscape rising. The Thuringian Forest on both sides of the A71: the road cutting through deciduous forest, the gradient present, the road curving. The A4 through Erfurt: the city at the centre of the state, the cathedral hill and the Severi church towers visible from the motorway approach before the road descends into the urban section. The Rennsteig - the ridge road along the Thuringian Forest watershed - crosses the A71 at one point. It is the oldest documented road in Germany. The motorway crosses it and continues.

## Holds
No roads in this project cross Thuringia yet.

## Offers
The A71 through the forest: demanding in winter, well-engineered, tunnels at the main passes. The A4 as the main east-west route, high truck density. Unrestricted on most open sections. The road surface generally good.

## Withheld
Weimar: 15 km from the A4 - Goethe, Schiller, Bauhaus, Buchenwald, all within 10 km of each other. Erfurt Cathedral and the Severikirche on their shared hill: visible from the A4 for approximately 12 seconds. The Wartburg at Eisenach: 3 km from the A4 exit, not visible from the motorway.
