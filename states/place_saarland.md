# Saarland
## Das Land, das Deutschland so klar gewählt hat, dass es zweimal wählen musste.

## Owner
- Project: Autobahn

## Shown
The shortest Autobahn network in Germany. The A6 and A620 form the primary routes; the state crossed in under 30 minutes in any direction. French road infrastructure visible approaching the border: the A4 autoroute, the signage bilingual then French. The former steel industry landscape along the A620 through Saarbrücken: the riverbank of the Saar, the former mills, the road at the water's edge in places.

## Holds
No roads in this project cross Saarland yet.

## Offers
Short distances - no point in the state is more than 40 km from France by Autobahn. Low truck density compared to major transit corridors. The French border 15 minutes from most exits. Speed limits through Saarbrücken urban section.

## Withheld
The Völklinger Hütte: the former iron and steel works on the Saar, visible from the A620 as an industrial cathedral - UNESCO World Heritage since 1994. The Saar river itself runs parallel to the A620 but is not consistently visible from the road. The French border: present as an institutional fact, absent as a physical barrier since 1995.
