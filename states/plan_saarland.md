# Autobahn Build Plan: Saarland

*v1.0 — 03.05.2026*

## Status: STUB

Holds empty. Saarland is the smallest state in the plan — crossed in under 30 minutes
in any direction. France is 15 minutes from every exit. The state voted twice
to be part of Germany. The road does not reflect this.

## Defining Withheld

The Voelklinger Huette: UNESCO World Heritage since 1994, visible from A620 as an industrial cathedral.
The Saar river: parallel to the A620, not consistently visible.
France: present as an institution, absent as a barrier since Schengen 1995.
The 1955 referendum: the Saar voted to join Germany.
The 1935 referendum before it: the Saar voted to join Nazi Germany.
Both results are in the same soil the road sits on.
