## Defining Withheld

Wittenberg: 30 km south of the A9. The church door where the Reformation began.
The Elbe crossing near Magdeburg: water in the flat landscape, briefly, then gone.
Magdeburg Cathedral: visible on the horizon from the A2 bridge. Twin towers. Then gone.
The state as transit. The road has accommodated this for seventy years.
