# Sachsen
## Die Menschen, die Geschichte verändert haben und noch warten, dass Geschichte es bemerkt.

## Owner
- Project: Autobahn

## Shown
The A4 through the Saxon uplands: the landscape climbing from the flat east into the Erzgebirge foothills west of Chemnitz. Dresden approached from the west - the Elbe crossing, the bridge briefly showing the city: Frauenkirche rebuilt, the Altstadt skyline, then gone. The A17 south toward Prague: the road ascending toward the Czech border through narrow valley.

## Holds
No roads in this project cross Saxony yet.

## Offers
Good road surface throughout. The A4 is the main east-west axis of southern Germany and runs continuously from the French border to the Polish border. Traffic moderate to heavy on the Dresden-Leipzig corridor. The A17 to Prague is an international transit route; freight heavy.

## Withheld
Dresden from the A4 bridge: the Frauenkirche and the Altstadt visible for approximately 8 seconds at motorway speed. The Volkswagen Gläserne Manufaktur in Dresden city centre: visible from neither the A4 nor the A17. The Erzgebirge ridge: visible from the A4 west of Chemnitz, not accessible from the carriageway. The Leipzig trade fair grounds: 3 km from the A14 exit, not visible from the motorway.
