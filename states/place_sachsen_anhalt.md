# Sachsen-Anhalt
## Das Land, das veränderte, wie Europa betet - und dann aufhörte zu beten.

## Owner
- Project: Autobahn

## Shown
The A2 at full width - the main east-west artery of northern Germany, running straight through the state. Flat. The Elbe crossing near Magdeburg: a moment of water in the flat landscape. Magdeburg Cathedral visible on clear days from the A2 bridge approach - the twin towers on the horizon, briefly, then gone. The road through the state is predominantly transit. The state is on the way to somewhere else. The road knows this. The road does not mind.

## Holds
No roads in this project cross Saxony-Anhalt yet.

## Offers
The A2 is the primary route; well-maintained, high truck density (the main east-west freight corridor), speed limits only in construction zones. The A9 north-south through the state, also unrestricted on most sections. The surface shows age in places - maintenance investment lower than western states.

## Withheld
Wittenberg: 30 km south of the A9 at the right exit - the church door where the Reformation began. Magdeburg Cathedral: visible from the A2 at distance; not reachable without leaving the motorway. The Saale river valley west of Halle: 10 km from the A9, the road gives no indication it is there.
