# A20

## Owner
- Project: Autobahn

## Shown
The Ostseeautobahn. The A20 runs east from its current western terminus at Weede - through the Segeberger Forst, past Kreuz Lübeck where the A1 crosses, and east through Groß Sarau into Mecklenburg-Vorpommern. From there it continues past Wismar, Rostock, and Stralsund toward the Polish border. The SH section runs from the planned A21 junction at Bad Segeberg-Nord to the state line east of Groß Sarau - nine stops across 43 kilometres.

## Offers
The Baltic coast corridor. The connection between Lübeck and the Mecklenburg-Vorpommern coast.

## Withheld
The western extension. The A20 ends at Weede. The planned continuation through Schleswig-Holstein toward Hamburg has been contested, legally challenged, and intermittently revived for decades. The road that was supposed to be here is not.
