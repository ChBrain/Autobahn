## Shown
The current western terminus of the A20 in Schleswig-Holstein. The road ends here - to the west the B206 continues toward Bad Segeberg. Extension to Bad Segeberg and beyond planned and contested for decades. The A20 stops here and the traffic disperses.

## Offers

## Withheld
The salt beneath. The ground here sits above the Segeberg salt diapir - an ancient column of salt pushing slowly upward through the glacial deposits of the North German Plain. Nearby at Bad Segeberg it broke through the surface, forming the Kalkberg. The rest of it runs underground, invisible, beneath the fields around the road terminus.
