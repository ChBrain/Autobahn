# Raststätte Kronberg

## Owner
- Project: Autobahn
- Place: [A20](place_a20.md) in [Schleswig-Holstein](place_schleswig_holstein.md)


## Shown
A rest stop in the Segeberger Forst. Trees on both sides. The road here running through the middle of the forest between Weede and Geschendorf - the longest gap between exits on the SH section.

## Holds
[km 7.9](piece_the_kilometerstein.md): Raststätte Kronberg.
- 3.5 km west: [Weede](place_a20_weede.md)
- 4.8 km east: [Geschendorf](place_a20_geschendorf.md)


## Offers
Rastplatz - the rest stop. Parking, facilities. No exit to the ordinary road.


## Withheld
Before the A20 was built through here, archaeologists worked the route for years. Near Bad Segeberg they found a large Iron Age urn cemetery - the dead of a community buried in the ground at the edge of what is now the Segeberger Forst, undisturbed through centuries of farming above them. The road was built anyway. The urns that could be reached were documented first.
