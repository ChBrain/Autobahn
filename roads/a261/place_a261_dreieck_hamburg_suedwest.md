The city edge visible to the north; the forested hills to the south. The Hamburg-Niedersachsen boundary crossing the carriageway within 300 metres of the fork.

## Withheld
The Staatsforst Rosengarten - marked with boundary stones since the 14th century - begins directly south of the junction on the Niedersachsen side. Of the original approximately 246 stones, 176 survive, standing along forest tracks, listed as cultural monuments. The forest and its stones begin where Hamburg ends. The road does not indicate either.
