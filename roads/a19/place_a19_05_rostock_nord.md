## Withheld
The Neptun Werft is 800 metres from this exit. Founded in 1850, it built warships for the Kaiser, was bombed in 1942, rebuilt by the DDR, and spent thirty years producing vessels for the Soviet merchant fleet. After 1990 it was sold, restructured, and retooled for cruise ship module construction - the prefabricated cabin blocks that Meyer Werft assembles in Papenburg. The workforce went from 8,000 to under 800. The cranes are still there. The shipyard is still there. The road passes without a sign.
