# Rostock-Nord

## Owner
- Project: Autobahn
- Place: [A19](place_a19.md) in [Mecklenburg-Vorpommern](place_mecklenburg_vorpommern.md)

## Shown
The exit into the northern residential districts of Rostock. Still within the city limits. The road separates port from city proper. Four-lane divided highway. The landscape has not changed - still flat, still open, still the North European plain. Another exit, another sign, another moment of choosing to leave the flow.

## Offers
Ausfahrt 5 - exit into Rostock north, local traffic toward the city centre and satellite neighbourhoods.

## Withheld
The socialist planning that shaped these districts. The Plattenbau - the prefabricated concrete apartment blocks that housed the workforce. The post-1990 transformation. The Warnow river that the city refuses to fully show from the motorway.
