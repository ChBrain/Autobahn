## Withheld
The University of Rostock was founded in 1419 -- the oldest university in the Baltic region and one of the oldest in the German-speaking world. Its main building stands 2 kilometres from this exit. The university was closed twice before it turned 300: once by the plague in 1437, when the faculty and students dispersed to Greifswald and did not return for years; once by the Swedes in 1628 during the Thirty Years War. Both times it reopened. It is still open.
