# Kessin

## Owner
- Project: Autobahn
- Place: [A19](place_a19.md) in [Mecklenburg-Vorpommern](place_mecklenburg_vorpommern.md)

## Shown
Bundesstraße 103 junction in the outer Rostock region. Flat agricultural land. The first exit where the landscape is entirely rural - no city edge visible anymore. Forest to the east. Fields to the west. A19 now fully a regional connector.

## Offers
Ausfahrt 8 - Bundesstraße 103, connection toward outer regional districts and small towns.

## Withheld
The settlements inland from the motorway, unreachable without leaving it. The local roads, the village life, the agricultural rhythm that the motorway passes through but never shows.
