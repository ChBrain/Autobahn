# Dreieck Wittstock/Dosse

## Owner
- Project: Autobahn
- Place: [A19](place_a19.md) in [Brandenburg](place_brandenburg.md)

## Shown
The junction where A19 terminates into A24. Dreieck Wittstock/Dosse - the end of the Rostock-to-Berlin corridor. The road geometry shifts: the A19 flow merges with the A24 east-west traffic toward Berlin and toward the Polish border. The three-level interchange. The decision point: Berlin west or Berlin east via the ring.

## Holds
[km 128](piece_the_kilometerstein.md): Dreieck Wittstock/Dosse.
* [A24](place_a24.md) connects here to Berlin.
* [Wittstock](place_a19_20_wittstock.md) is north on [A19](place_a19.md).

## Offers
Exit 21 - A24 interchange. Europastraße 55/E26 routing. Connection to Berlin's orbital network and onward to Poland.

## Withheld
The A24 itself - the continuation of Europastraße 55 toward the Polish border. The traffic volumes that justify this infrastructure convergence. The political geometry: the road that the DDR began, that German reunification completed, that now connects North Sea ports to Eastern Europe.
