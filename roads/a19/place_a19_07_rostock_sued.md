## Withheld
In August 1992, three kilometres from this exit, a crowd attacked the Sunflower House -- the Sonnenblumenhaus -- a residential block housing Vietnamese contract workers and asylum seekers in the Lichtenhagen district. The attacks lasted four days. The building burned. Residents were trapped on the roof. Several hundred people watched from the street and did not intervene. The police did not intervene. It was the worst racist violence in postwar German history. A plaque was added to the building in 2012. The motorway does not mention it.
