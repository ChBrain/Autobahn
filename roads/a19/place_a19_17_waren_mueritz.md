# Waren (Müritz)

## Owner
- Project: Autobahn
- Place: [A19](place_a19.md) in [Mecklenburg-Vorpommern](place_mecklenburg_vorpommern.md)

## Shown
Bundesstraße 192 junction. Waren is a larger lake town - the Müritz, Germany's largest lake, visible or accessible from this exit. The major lake destination from Berlin, from Hamburg, from the north. Waren is the established tourism centre. The motorway announces it clearly. Summer weekends: the traffic surge southbound from Berlin toward the Müritz beaches.
