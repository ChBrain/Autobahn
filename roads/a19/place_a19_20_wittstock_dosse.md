## Shown
Bundesstraße 189 junction. Wittstock/Dosse: the end of the 24-kilometre gap, the Brandenburg border crossed. Exit 20 on the A19. The A24 connection lies a few kilometres ahead. Still flat, still straight. The landscape has not changed - flat German plateau, the result of ice age glaciation, offering no elevation, no surprise, no visual anchor.
