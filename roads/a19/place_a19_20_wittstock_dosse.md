# Wittstock

## Owner
- Project: Autobahn
- Place: [A19](place_a19.md) in [Brandenburg](place_brandenburg.md)

## Shown
Bundesstraße 189 junction. Wittstock: the end of the 24-kilometre gap, the Brandenburg border crossed. Exit 20 on the A19. The town lies inland but the road passes through its regional network. Still flat, still straight. The landscape has not changed - flat German plateau, the result of ice age glaciation, offering no elevation, no surprise, no visual anchor.

## Offers
Ausfahrt 20 - Bundesstraße 189 toward Wittstock/Dosse and Brandenburg interior. The choice to leave the motorway after the gap. Regional connection to A24 interchange ahead.

## Withheld
The gap itself - the 24 kilometres where no one can stop except in emergency. The longest motorway segment in Germany without an exit. The political decision-making that created this void. The traffic that must commit to the full 24 km or exit at Röbel/Müritz.
