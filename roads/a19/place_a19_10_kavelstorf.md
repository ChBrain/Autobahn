# Kavelstorf

## Owner
- Project: Autobahn
- Place: [A19](place_a19.md) in [Mecklenburg-Vorpommern](place_mecklenburg_vorpommern.md)

## Shown
The village exit. South of the Kreuz Rostock interchange, the A19 enters its true rural character. Kavelstorf is unremarkable on any normal day - a sign, a road, the continuation south. But on April 8, 2011, this location became catastrophic: a sandsturm from the Baltic, reduced visibility to metres, 85 vehicles in both directions collided in seconds, 30 caught fire, a Hazmat truck burning, 8 dead. The Massenkarambolage - the multi-car crash that was Germany's worst traffic disaster. The road still carries the memory.
