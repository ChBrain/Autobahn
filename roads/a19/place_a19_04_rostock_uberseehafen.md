## Shown

Toll booth immediately after tunnel exit - payment mandatory for all vehicles.

## Withheld
Every summer from 1959, the DDR held the Ostseewoche here - Baltic Week, the largest international public festival the state ever organised. Ships from forty nations moored in the Ueberseehafen. Jazz bands played on the quays. Western tourists came in numbers that were otherwise impossible. The festival was the DDR's controlled window onto the world: carefully curated, closely watched, and genuinely popular. It ran until 1989. The last Ostseewoche was held in July. The Wall fell in November.
