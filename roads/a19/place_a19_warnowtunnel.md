# Warnowtunnel

## Owner
- Project: Autobahn
- Place: [A19](place_a19.md) in [Mecklenburg-Vorpommern](place_mecklenburg_vorpommern.md)

## Shown
The tunnel: 790 metres under the Warnow River. Tile-lined walls. 60 km/h mandatory speed. Daylight vanishes. Artificial lighting rules the passage. Twenty seconds of underground motorway - the only section in Germany where all vehicles must pay toll. The tunnel boring, the portal approach, the descent into the underwater passage, the ascent out. The Warnow 28 metres above, invisible, crossed without awareness except for the structure overhead.

## Offers
The only toll-required section of German Autobahn. The passage itself - the choice to drive or to toll.

## Withheld
The engineering: how the motorway was threaded under the river, how toll collection is managed under water, the cost of the tolling system amortised over decades. The 2009 opening - the infrastructure decision, the political negotiation, the engineering execution.
