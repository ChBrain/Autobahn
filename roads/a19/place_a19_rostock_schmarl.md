## Withheld
Schmarl was a village before it was a district. Documented since the 14th century, it was incorporated into Rostock in 1950 as the DDR expanded the city's administrative boundary. The farmland was built over with Plattenbau housing blocks through the 1970s to accommodate the shipyard workforce. When the Warnowtunnel was planned in the 1990s, the approach road consumed the last open land at the village's southern edge. The name survived. The village did not.
