# Röbel/Müritz

## Owner
- Project: Autobahn
- Place: [A19](place_a19.md) in [Mecklenburg-Vorpommern](place_mecklenburg_vorpommern.md)

## Shown
Bundesstraße 198 junction. Röbel/Müritz is the last exit in Mecklenburg-Vorpommern on the A19. After this: 24 kilometres with no exits - the longest exit-free stretch in Germany. The landscape still flat, still lake-dominated. The road continues straight south, but this is the last chance to leave the motorway in MV. Beyond this: emptiness measured in kilometres, the planned Wredenhagen exit never built, the Brandenburg border, and finally Wittstock/Dosse in Brandenburg.

## Holds
[km 77.0](piece_the_kilometerstein.md): Röbel/Müritz.
* [Waren (Müritz)](place_a19_17_waren_mueritz.md) is north on [A19](place_a19.md).
* Gap: 24 km to [Wittstock/Dosse](place_a19_20_wittstock_dosse.md) in Brandenburg.

## Offers
Ausfahrt 18 - Bundesstraße 198 toward Röbel and final Müritz access. The last exit before Brandenburg. The choice: leave now or commit to the 24 km gap.

## Withheld
The planned Wredenhagen exit - announced, never built. The planning decision to leave that stretch empty. The traffic that cannot stop there. The geography that remains disconnected by motorway design.
