# A26

## Owner
- Project: Autobahn

## Shown
Partially open, Hamburg section under construction until at least 2028. The A26 runs west from Hamburg through the Altes Land and the Elbe marshes to Stade and toward Drochtersen on the lower Elbe. The Niedersachsen section from Neu Wulmstorf to Stade is in service. The final 8.6 km into Kreuz Hamburg-Hafen remains under construction. The western extension to Drochtersen and the A20 is planned but not yet built.

## Offers
The route south of the Elbe: connecting the lower Elbe marshes and the Altes Land to Hamburg without the B73. Port access for Stade. Traffic distribution across the Kehdingen region once the western extension is complete.

## Withheld
The A20 Elbe tunnel at Drochtersen is the missing piece. Until it exists, the A26 has no western anchor. The Kreuz Kehdingen cannot be built until the tunnel delivers traffic to meet it. The A26 waits at both ends: at Drochtersen for the A20, at Hamburg-Hafen for the A1. A motorway 50 km long with unfinished termini at each end.
