## Withheld
Archaeological finds at former harbour sites near Drochtersen and Oederquart show close and well-documented contact with the Roman Empire: coins, vessels, weapons, jewellery moved through here along the Elbe. Recent finds along the river also confirm Viking presence. The Elbe at this point was the oldest trade route into this landscape -- used by Romans and Norsemen before any road existed here. The planned motorway interchange is being built at the place where those routes began. The Kreuz Kehdingen does not exist yet. The Elbe still does.
