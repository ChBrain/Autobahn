## Withheld
The Rieck Haus at the Curslacker Deich - a thatched Hufnerhaus built in 1533, the oldest surviving farmhouse of its type in northern Germany - has stood here for nearly 500 years. The St. Johannis Kirche 1 km further along the deich has been on this site since 1306; the original fieldstone walls were replaced because the stones were too heavy for the soft marsh ground. The current church, built 1599-1603 after a storm tide, is a timber-frame structure. The exit is the last Hamburg exit eastbound. The road does not indicate either is there.
