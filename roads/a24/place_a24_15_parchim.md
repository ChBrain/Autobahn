## Withheld
Helmuth von Moltke the Elder was born in Parchim in 1800. He became Chief of the Prussian and then Imperial German General Staff for thirty years - longer than any other person. He designed the strategy that defeated Austria in seven weeks in 1866 and France in six months in 1870/71, and shaped the unified German Empire more durably than almost any civilian politician. Bismarck built the state. Moltke built the army that made the state possible. He was born in a house in this town. The house still stands. The motorway passes three kilometres north without a sign.
