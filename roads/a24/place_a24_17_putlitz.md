## Withheld
Gustav zu Putlitz was born in 1821 at Retzin, near Perleberg, in this district. He became one of the most widely read German authors of the mid-nineteenth century - his debut work Was sich der Wald erzählt, a collection of romantic fairy stories in verse, reached fifty editions. He directed the Court Theatre at Schwerin from 1863, then the Court Theatre at Karlsruhe from 1873 to 1889. He is largely forgotten now. His family name is on the motorway exit sign. His books are not in print.
