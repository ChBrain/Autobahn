## Offers
Ausfahrt 16 - the exit ramp. Access to Suckow village and the Ruhner Berge.

## Withheld
A few kilometres south, the Ruhner Berge rise to 178 metres - the second highest point in Mecklenburg-Vorpommern. In a landscape this flat, 178 metres is a mountain. The hills are a glacial formation, moraine pushed up by the last ice sheet and left when it retreated. From the top on a clear day the Schwerin lake district is visible to the north and the Prignitz flatlands to the south. The motorway passes the edge of this landscape without a sign.
