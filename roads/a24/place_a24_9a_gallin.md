## Withheld
Gallin was first recorded in 1230. Later it belonged, together with the villages of Bernstorf, Camin, Neuenkirchen, and Tessin, to the Züle family - classified in contemporary documents as Raubritter, robber knights. In 1349 they were expelled by a coalition of Duke Erich of Saxony, the Count of Holstein, and the city of Hamburg. Three powers combined to remove one family from one cluster of villages. Whatever they were doing here required a coalition to stop.
