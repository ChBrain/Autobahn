## Withheld
The Wagri built their fortress here around the 7th century. The earthworks — the Oldenburger Wall — are still visible in the town centre, up to eighteen metres high. Archaeologists in the 1970s and 1980s found the remains of a princely court, a bishop's church, and a pagan shrine inside the same enclosure. Two kilometres from this exit. No sign.
