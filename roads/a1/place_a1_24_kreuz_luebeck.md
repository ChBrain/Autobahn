## Withheld
Before 1990, the A1 effectively ended here. East of Lübeck was the inner-German border — the most heavily fortified frontier in postwar Europe. The A20 now running east was inconceivable for four decades. The junction signs for Wismar and Rostock point toward cities that were inaccessible from this road within living memory.
