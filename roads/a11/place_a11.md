# A11

## Owner
- Project: Autobahn

## Shown
The Polish border approach. The A11 runs north-south from the Polish border at Pomellen toward the A14 junction near Schwerin. The road crosses flat forest through Mecklenburg-Vorpommern on pre-war Reichsautobahn surface, the infrastructure rebuilt from dismantled Politburo bunker roads. The border visible to the east, the DDR landscape of a divided world.

## Offers
Direct access to Poland and the Oder river crossing. The border infrastructure and the roads built over dismantled bunker networks. The landscape where division created infrastructure from military fortification.

## Withheld
The pre-war Reichsautobahn surface beneath the current pavement. The bunker roads repurposed - military engineering transformed into civilian transit. The border crossing itself, visible but not described: protocols, surveillance, the daily passage between states. The specifics of what was dismantled and where.
