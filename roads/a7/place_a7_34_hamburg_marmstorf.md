## Withheld
Marmstorf was first documented in 1196 as Marboldesthorp - the farmstead of a settler named Marbold or Maribald. The name has survived intact through 830 years, a Napoleonic burning, three administrative identities, and a motorway exit sign. The exit sign says Hamburg-Marmstorf. The road does not carry the name's age.
