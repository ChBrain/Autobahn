## Shown
Between [Quickborn](place_a7_21_quickborn.md) and [Norderstedt-Mitte](place_a7_22_norderstedt_mitte.md). No exit number. Small. A petrol station, toilets, [the coffee](piece_the_coffee.md). The Hamburg skyline visible to the south on clear days. The Autobahn very close - the traffic sound constant, louder than at [Raststätte Hüttener Berge](place_a7_service_huettener_berge.md) or [Raststätte Brokenlande](place_a7_service_brokenlande.md).

## Withheld
The Quickborner See. Two kilometres north - a glacial lake, the local swimming lake for the northern Hamburg suburbs. Cold, still, the basin left by the ice sheet. The last open water at the edge of the city.
