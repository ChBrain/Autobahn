## Withheld
Louis Harms. Born in Walsrode on 5 May 1808, the son of the local pastor - one kilometre from this exit. Known as the Erwecker der Heide - the Awakener of the Heath. He founded the Hermannsburger Mission in 1849 and in 1853 commissioned a ship, the Kandaze, built from donations by Heide farmers and tradespeople, which sailed missionaries to Zululand in southern Africa. It made thirteen voyages. The police in Hermannsburg reportedly noted they needed two fewer gendarmes after he began preaching there. A single memorial stone stands on the church square in Walsrode. The exit reaches it.
