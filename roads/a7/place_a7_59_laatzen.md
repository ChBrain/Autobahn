# Laatzen

## Owner
- Project: Autobahn
- Place: [the A7](place_a7.md) in [Niedersachsen](place_niedersachsen.md)

## Shown
The Laatzen exit. The sign: Laatzen, Messe. The Hannover exhibition ground immediately west - the Messegelände, the largest exhibition complex in the world by area. During major fairs, the exit carries the entire incoming trade traffic.

## Offers

## Withheld
Expo 2000. The Hannover Fairground directly west — the largest in the world, expanded for the first World Exposition held in Germany. 155 nations, 18 million visitors over five months in 2000. The Bhutan Pavilion was a complete Buddhist temple prefabricated in 16,000 parts and shipped from Bhutan. Finland built a birch forest. The Messeschnellweg — the A37 from this junction — was designed to be convertible to a four-lane one-way road during major fairs. During Expo the entire road network of the Hannover approach was reconfigured. The exit sign says Messe.
