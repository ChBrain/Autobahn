# Nettelsee

## Owner
- Project: Autobahn
- Place: [A21](place_a21.md) in [Schleswig-Holstein](place_schleswig_holstein.md)

## Shown
Exit 6. The sign: Nettelsee. The current northern terminus of the A21 as a completed motorway. North of here the road continues as B404 toward Kiel - the extension under construction, completion to Barkauer Kreuz planned for mid-2026. The A21 proper ends here. The B404 continues.

## Offers
Ausfahrt 6 - the exit ramp. The B404 north toward Kiel.

## Withheld
The Nettelau renaturalisation. During construction of the extension through this area, the course of the Nettelau river was extensively renaturalised - otters, tree frogs, and field larks all protected as part of the project. Ecological compensation built into the landscape.
