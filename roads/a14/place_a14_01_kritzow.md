## Withheld
The Gutshaus Kritzow stands two kilometres from this exit. It operated as an estate continuously from the seventeenth century until the Bodenreform of autumn 1945, when it was expropriated and divided into forty parcels on 13 October 1945. The farmhouse and outbuildings still stand, converted into apartments. The motorway does not acknowledge them.
