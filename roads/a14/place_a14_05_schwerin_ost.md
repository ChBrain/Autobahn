## Withheld
The Schloss stands on an island in the Burgsee, separated from the city by water on all sides. It has been a fortified site since at least 973 - first a Slavic stronghold of the Obotrites, then a German fortress after Henry the Lion took it in 1160, rebuilt continuously over six hundred years. The current building dates from the 1843 to 1857 reconstruction. The island beneath it is older than any of the structures on it.
