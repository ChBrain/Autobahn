# A23

## Owner
- Project: Autobahn

## Shown
Two lanes per direction for the full length - narrower than the A7. The Westküstenautobahn: 89 km from Heide to [Dreieck Hamburg-Nordwest](place_a23_22_dreieck_hamburg_nordwest.md) where it meets [the A7](place_a7.md). The landscape: flat, agricultural, the North Sea coast and the marshes of Dithmarschen to the west. The Kiel Canal crossed at Schafstedt - the bridge 56 metres high, 360 metres long. The road two-lane in places, rural in character despite being a motorway. The gap between [Itzehoe-Nord](place_a23_08_itzehoe_nord.md) and [Itzehoe-Süd](place_a23_10_itzehoe_sued.md): a section still Bundesstraße, not yet motorway. North of Heide: the road becomes the B5. The Autobahn ends and the west coast continues regardless.

## Offers
Continuous movement between Heide and Hamburg-Nordwest. The west coast of Schleswig-Holstein connected to the Hamburg motorway network. An alternative to the A7 for drivers coming from or going to the Nordsee coast.

## Withheld
The direction. The A23 runs both ways. Which direction a Driver is travelling is not visible in the road - only in the car and the person in it.
