# Leave Office

## Owner
- **Project:** REMEMBER
- **Process:** [Govern Traffic](process_govern_traffic.md)

## Initiated by
One of three conditions: election lost, coalition collapsed, resignation forced. The [Bundesminister fur Verkehr](../positions/position_bundesminister_fuer_verkehr_1949_1998.md) is vacated. The minister is no longer the minister.

## Direction
Towards the handover. Briefing files prepared for the successor at [Bundesverkehrsministerium Bonn](../places/place_bonn_robert_schuman_platz.md). Pending commitments documented. The ministry continues without the person who was running it.

## Lever
The carrying force: institutional continuity. The ministry does not stop when the minister leaves.

The resistance: the minister's own legacy calculus. What gets documented, what gets buried, what gets spun. The gap between what the handover files say and what the successor actually finds.

## Echo
[Govern Traffic](process_govern_traffic.md) runs again with a new minister. Pieces like [Federal Motorway Network + Road Transport Regulation](../pieces/piece_federal_motorway_network.md) transfer without consent. The next minister inherits them regardless of whether they would have chosen them.
