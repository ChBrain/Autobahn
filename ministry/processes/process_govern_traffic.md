# Govern Traffic

## Owner
- **Project:** REMEMBER

## Initiated by
A federal election produces a coalition agreement. The coalition agreement names a minister. The minister is sworn in. The [Bundesminister fur Verkehr](../positions/position_bundesminister_fuer_verkehr_1949_1998.md) exists and must be filled. The position does not wait.

## Direction
Towards a functioning national transport network: [Federal Motorway Network + Road Transport Regulation](../pieces/piece_federal_motorway_network.md) extended, rails funded, policy set, budget allocated from [Bundesverkehrsministerium Bonn](../places/place_bonn_robert_schuman_platz.md). The minister moves from appointment to commitment to consequence via [Take Office](process_take_office.md), [Run Office](process_run_office.md), and [Leave Office](process_leave_office.md).

## Lever
The carrying force: constitutional mandate held by the [Bundesminister fur Verkehr](../positions/position_bundesminister_fuer_verkehr_1949_1998.md), backed by the second-largest ministry budget in the federal government, exercised from [Bundesverkehrsministerium Bonn](../places/place_bonn_robert_schuman_platz.md).

The resistance: coalition partners, Bundesrat, EU regulation, lobby interests, Bundesbahn/DB AG, Autobahn condition, public opinion, and the gap between what the budget promises and what the network requires.

Remove either side and the process produces press releases, not policy.

## Echo
A tenure ends. The minister leaves a network in better or worse condition than they found it. The next minister inherits the commitments, the debts, the unfinished projects, and pieces like [Federal Motorway Network + Road Transport Regulation](../pieces/piece_federal_motorway_network.md) that cannot be undone.
