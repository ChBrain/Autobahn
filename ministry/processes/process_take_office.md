# Take Office

## Owner
- **Project:** REMEMBER
- **Process:** [Govern Traffic](process_govern_traffic.md)

## Initiated by
Coalition agreement signed. Chancellor issues Organisationserlass naming the minister. The [Bundesminister fur Verkehr](../positions/position_bundesminister_fuer_verkehr_1949_1998.md) is vacant and a name has been assigned to it.

## Direction
Towards the first working day. The minister crosses the threshold of [Bundesverkehrsministerium Bonn](../places/place_bonn_robert_schuman_platz.md), reads the briefing files left by the predecessor, and discovers what they have actually inherited.

## Lever
The carrying force: political mandate, party support, public expectation.

The resistance: the ministry's permanent staff, the predecessor's unfinished commitments, pieces like [Federal Motorway Network + Road Transport Regulation](../pieces/piece_federal_motorway_network.md) already in motion that the new minister did not choose and cannot easily stop.

## Echo
The minister is now in office. [Run Office](process_run_office.md) begins. The briefing files define the first six months whether the minister reads them or not.
