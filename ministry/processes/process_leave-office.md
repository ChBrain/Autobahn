# Leave Office

## Owner
- **Project:** REMEMBER
- **Process:** [Govern Traffic](process_govern-traffic.md)

## Initiated by
One of three conditions: election lost, coalition collapsed, resignation forced. The [Bundesminister fur Verkehr](../positions/position_bundesminister-fuer-verkehr-1949-1998.md) is vacated. The minister is no longer the minister.

## Direction
Towards the handover. Briefing files prepared for the successor at [Bundesverkehrsministerium Bonn](../places/place_bonn-robert-schuman-platz.md). Pending commitments documented. The ministry continues without the person who was running it.

## Lever
The carrying force: institutional continuity. The ministry does not stop when the minister leaves.

The resistance: the minister's own legacy calculus. What gets documented, what gets buried, what gets spun. The gap between what the handover files say and what the successor actually finds.

## Echo
[Govern Traffic](process_govern-traffic.md) runs again with a new minister. Pieces like [Federal Motorway Network + Road Transport Regulation](../pieces/piece_federal-motorway-network.md) transfer without consent. The next minister inherits them regardless of whether they would have chosen them.
