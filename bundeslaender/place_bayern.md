# Bayern
## Der Freistaat, der beigetreten ist - und nicht vergessen hat, dass es eine Wahl war.

## Owner
- Project: Autobahn

## Shown
The unrestricted sign. White on white background - the end of all speed restrictions. Not universal but frequent. The landscape shifting south: the pre-Alpine hills appearing on the A8 toward Salzburg. The Autobahnkirche at the roadside on several routes - a small chapel, a cross, a parking space. Present without explanation.

Munich from the ring roads: the BMW headquarters four-cylinder tower visible from the A9 approach. The Alps on clear days from the A8 south - a full wall of white on the horizon, not distant, present.

## Holds
No roads in this project cross Bavaria yet.

## Offers
Unrestricted speed on approximately 60% of Bavarian motorways - the highest proportion of any state. Well-maintained surface. The Alpine sections of the A8 and A95 demanding in winter: snow, ice, gradient. Autobahn service areas well-spaced with higher quality than German average.

## Withheld
The Alps: present on the horizon from most of southern Bavaria but unreachable from the motorway. Neuschwanstein: 10 km from the B17 exit off the A7 extension if it reaches Füssen. The BMW Welt in Munich: 2 km from the A9 at Munich-Schwabing. The beer garden culture: not visible from, and not accessible on, the A-road network.
