# Mecklenburg-Vorpommern

## Owner
- Project: Autobahn

## Shown
Flat. The pine forests close. The road quiet by western German standards. The sky large. The signs on the A20: Rostock, Stralsund, Greifswald. Ferry connections east: Trelleborg, Gdynia, Helsinki. The road used seasonally for Baltic coast tourism - in summer, denser; in winter, close to empty. The landscape does not withhold anything. There is simply more of it between points.

## Holds
No roads in this project cross Mecklenburg-Vorpommern yet.

## Offers
Low traffic density - among the lowest of any German state. The A20 runs the full east-west length of the state, well-surfaced. Unrestricted speed on most sections. The road gives back what the terrain withholds: straightness, space, distance.

## Withheld
The Baltic coast - within 30 km of most of the A20, not visible from it. The Bodden lagoons east of Stralsund: the road crosses the Stralsund bridge and the water is briefly present. The former fishing industry. The empty villages 5 km from the motorway exits, where the population declined after 1990 and has not returned.
